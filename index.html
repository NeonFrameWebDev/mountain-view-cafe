Our Place, In a Minute

A scratch kitchen and a bakery case, tucked off Duval.

In 2014, Nancy Byrd and her late husband opened a small, family-owned lunch cafe in Green Valley. The idea was simple. Scratch-cook the sandwiches, bake the bread and the desserts in-house, take care of every regular like you want to see them come back.

Twelve years later, that is still exactly what we do. Come for a burger, leave with a slice of cheesecake and a prickly pear iced tea to-go.
